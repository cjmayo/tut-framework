credits and kudosAuthors

Template Unit Test framework was designed and written by Vladimir Dyuzhev, who currently lives in Toronto, Canada. His personal site is vladimir-dyuzhev.net

In recent years TUT was part-time maintained by Denis Kononenko.

Since 2008 it’s maintained and developed by Michał Rzechonek.

We would like to thank all contributors, especially Nokia Siemens Networks IPT CS team for extensive real world testing.
